# honeybee_energy_revive/ — REVIVE extensions to honeybee-energy

Phius-REVIVE attributes on Honeybee-Energy objects (constructions, HVAC). Registered via `_extend_honeybee_energy_revive.py`. IPy2.7-safe.

## Contents

- `_extend_honeybee_energy_revive.py` — registers the REVIVE slot onto honeybee-energy objects.
- `hvac/` — REVIVE HVAC equipment attributes.
- `properties/` — per-host-object property classes (own serialization).

## Notes
- Backward-compatible HBJSON serialization required. Tests: `../tests/`.
